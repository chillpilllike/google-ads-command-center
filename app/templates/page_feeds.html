How campaigns should use these feeds

Stable feed names
Use the feed name shown below in Google Ads Business data. Campaigns should attach that feed name without the .csv extension.
Separate campaign lanes
Best-products campaigns attach the best feed, good-products campaigns attach the good feed, and discovery campaigns attach the new feed.
When products move
A product promoted into good or best appears in that feed on the next generation. Keep campaign-to-feed mapping stable.
Refresh behavior
If Google is configured to fetch the URL on a schedule, the same file URL can refresh. If it was uploaded once manually, create or update the feed in Google Ads again.
